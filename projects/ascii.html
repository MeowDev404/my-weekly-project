ASCII Art Converter

A simple tool that converts normal text into big ASCII art. Inspired by terminal-style text effects.

  • Type text and convert instantly
  • Big readable ASCII alphabet
  • Auto line wrapping
  • Copy button for easy sharing

How to use?
Download the ASCII Art Converter below and open it.

Type any text in the input box and it will be converted into big ASCII art next to or below it.
